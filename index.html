About Us

Tru-Art Sign Co., Inc. is a full service company dedicated to serving the sign industry, outdoor advertising and commercial light trades since 1955. Manufacturing, Installation, Service, Surveys, and obtaining Permits are some of our specialties. We handle all of our fabrication and installations in a truly professional manner. Our knowledge and capabilities in the sign industry is unlimited due to our experienced staff of seasoned professionals. Tru-Art Sign Company has the ability to meet all of your signage needs. We have a full fleet of crane trucks, aerial ladder trucks and various other service vehicles to install and maintain your signage. We are able to design, manufacture, and install just about anything you can think of from building signage to monument signs.
